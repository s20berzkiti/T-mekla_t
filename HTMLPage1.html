Furbies

Furby is an American electronic robotic toy that was originally released in 1998 by Tiger Electronics. It resembles a hamster or owllike creature and went through a period of being a "must-have" toy following its holiday season launch, with continual sales until 2000.

In recent years this strange looking toy has found an occult following with the now creative adults, who used to love the toy as children. Starting in 2018 with the creation of the "long furby". Tumblr user furbyfuzz, also known as Aloe, created the first LongFurby, with the initial thought of "What if Furby was.... longer...". "She was an Idea that I just had to get out and onto paper". 5 months after drawing the concept, on May 14th, 2018, LongFurby was born. Aloe became famous within the furby community, and produced long furbys to sell. Many other people started making their own long furbys, developing a long furby community niche. There's even a dedicated website gallery for all the long furbies, longfurby.com.

This sparked not only a renewed interest in the original furbies but furbies of all kinds. Now almost any type of furby can be found, long furbies, round furbies, furnies with human proportions and so on. The novelty and creepy aspects of these one of a kind creations can drive their prices into the hundreds of dollars.

Making your own unique furby is a challenge of creativity and sowing.

Furby Eras

Era 1 furbies

Furby (1998) was produced and released from 1998 to 2002. Several Furbys were re-released with a sticker on the box titled "Fresh New Look!". Many of these Furbys had slight changes to their designs and are listed below as Fresh New Look Furbys.

Era 2 furbies

2005 saw the resurgence of a new Furby design, called Emoto-Tronic furbys, or Furby 2005. The Emoto-Tronic Furby Babies were released in 2005. The Funky Furbys were released in 2006.

Era 3 furbies

Seven years after the release of the Emoto-Tronic Furbys in 2005, another adult Furby model was released, with the slogan "A Mind Of Its Own".

Era 4 furbies

The Furby Connect was released in 2016. These were not as popular as their previous counterparts.
